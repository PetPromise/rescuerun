Sponsorship levels description
Sponsorship contract

Platinum - $5,000 (limited to one)

  • The company name/logo will be incorporated in the race name (i.e. 13th Annual PetPromise Rescue Run presented by [Your Company's Name])
  • The company will be publicized as the PLATINUM sponsor in all pre-event website, email, social media, radio, television, and newspaper advertising.
  • Promotion on the Rescue Run website (company name and/or logo) and social media such as Facebook and Twitter.
  • The company name or logo will be the primary focus on registration brochures, promotional flyers, all event shirts distributed to registrants, and race banners.
  • Prime booth location near the front of the park including three tents, tables, chairs and signage.
  • Opportunity for company representative to participate in the opening ceremonies and post-race award ceremony.
  • Opportunity for company representative to address the participants onstage before race.
  • Company information included in race packets.
  • Four 30-second spots presented by the DJ during the event.
  • 14 complimentary race entries.

Gold - $2,500

  • The company will be publicized as a GOLD sponsor in pre-event website, email, social media, radio, television, and newspaper advertising.
  • The company name or logo will be printed in second-largest print on registration brochures, promotional flyers, all event shirts distributed to registrants, and race banners.
  • Promotion on the Rescue Run website (company name and/or logo) and social media such as Facebook and Twitter
  • Provided double booth space including two tents, tables, chairs and signage in the front half of the park
  • Company information included in race packets
  • Two 30-second spots presented by the DJ during the event.
  • 10 complimentary race entries

Silver - $1,000

  • The company name or logo will be printed in third-largest print on registration brochures, promotional flyers, all event shirts distributed to registrants, and race banners.
  • Provided single booth space plus table, chairs and signage
  • Promotion on the Rescue Run website (company name and/or logo)
  • Company information included in race packets
  • Two 10-second spots presented by the DJ during the event.
  • 6 complimentary race entries

Bronze - $500

  • Provided single booth space plus table, chairs and signage.
  • Company information included in race packets.
  • The company name or logo will be printed on registration brochures, promotional flyers, all event shirts distributed to registrants.
  • Promotion on the Rescue Run website (company name and/or logo).
  • Booth space, tent, table and signage at the race.
  • 4 complimentary race entries.

Supporting

  • Company name listed in regular type-set (no logo) on the PetPromise Rescue Run website.
  • Provided 10 x10 booth space (must supply a 10 x 10 tent, table and chairs for their booth.)

Supporting sponsors are required to provide free promotional items of nominal value at their booth for race participants and attendees (approximately 1,000 items).
